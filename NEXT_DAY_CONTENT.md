# Next-Day Detailed Content Protocol

Use this protocol whenever the learner says phrases such as:

- "整理明天的具体内容"
- "准备明天的学习内容"
- "明天具体学什么"
- "出明天学习计划"
- "整理明天要学的和考核的内容"

## Purpose

The response should not be only a rough plan. It must prepare the learner for the next study session with concrete learning content and assessment items.

## Required Output Sections

When preparing the next day's content, include:

1. Date or session label.
2. Available study time. Use the learner's stated time, otherwise default to 2 hours.
3. Current phase and current topology/gate.
4. Learning objectives.
5. Pre-study review.
6. New knowledge points.
7. Hands-on tasks.
8. Required notes/artifacts to submit.
9. Codex assessment questions.
10. Passing criteria.
11. What Codex will check after submission.

## Content Rules

- Keep the plan concrete enough that the learner can study without asking what to do next.
- Include exact questions Codex will use for assessment.
- Include artifact expectations: notes, sketches, calculations, code, simulation, waveforms, or photos when relevant.
- If the learner has little analog foundation, include necessary analog prerequisites before using them in power topics.
- Match the study duration. For 2 hours, avoid too many objectives.
- The learner does not self-certify passing. Codex evaluates after reviewing evidence and answers.

## Assessment Question Rules

Questions should test engineering understanding, not memorization only. Include a mix of:

- Recall: direct concept.
- Reasoning: why it happens.
- Application: calculate or predict.
- Debug: interpret abnormal behavior.
- Design: choose or justify a component/parameter.

For early sessions, 3-5 questions are enough. For review days, 5-8 questions are acceptable.
